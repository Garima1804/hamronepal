Mt.Everest

Mount Everest is Earth's highest mountain above sea level, located in the Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the Himalayas. The Chinaâ€“Nepal border runs across its summit point. Its elevation of 8,848.86 m was most recently established in 2020 by the Nepali and Chinese authorities. Mt.Everest, is located inside the park core area (Sagarmatha National Park).The park was enlisted as the UNESCO World Heritage site in 1979 as the first UNESCO natural world heritage site of Nepal. Mt.Everest is believed to be more than 60 million years old.Thousands of people come to visit this place every year.New Zealander Sir Edmund Hillary and Nepali Sherpa Tenzing Norgay are credited as the first to summit Mount Everest in 1953. Prior to their ascent, numerous mountaineers attempted to summit Mount Everest, including George Mallory who discovered the north route to the summit in 1921 and George Finch, who reached an altitude of over 27,000 feet (8230m) using oxygen for the first time in 1922. Everest region in the north eastern province of Nepal,is in a world of its own with vast glaciers, icefalls, the highest mountains, deep valleys, precarious settlements, and hardy people challenging the harshest conditions thrown at them by nature in the thin air of high altitude.
