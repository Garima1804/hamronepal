Janakpurdham

Janakpurdham or Janakpur is a sub-metropolitan city in Dhanusa District, Province No. 2, Nepal. The city is a hub for religious and cultural tourism. It has been declared as the temporary capital for Province No. 2 until the Province Assembly votes for a permanent capital. Janakpur is an important pilgrimage site for Hindus all over Nepal and India. It is the birthplace of Goddess Janaki or Sita (Hindu epic-the Ramayana). In ancient times, Janakpur was the capital city of the Kingdom of Mithila and the centre of Maithil culture. It is the headquarter of Dhanusha District and is connected by roadways and airways to the capital city Kathmandu.
